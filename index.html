NigeBot’s Regulations

Each race weekend you predict the top 3 in qualifying and the top 3 in the Grand Prix. On Sprint weekends you also predict the top 3 in the Sprint Race. You also predict the fastest lap in the Grand Prix. Points are awarded as follows:

Predictions lock automatically when qualifying starts. Get your picks in before then — after that, no changes can be made.

✅ Exact match — if you predict a driver in the correct finishing position, you get the full points for that position.

🔁 One position off — if you predict a driver one place out (e.g. predict P1 and they finish P2), you get half of the points for where they actually finished.

❌ Two or more positions off — no points. If a driver doesn't finish in the top 3 at all, you get zero for that driver.

⚡ Fastest lap — 10 points if you correctly name the fastest-lap driver in the Grand Prix. There are no half-points for fastest lap; it's all-or-nothing.

🏁 Sprint Race — scored exactly like the main race but at half points (P1 12.5, P2 9, P3 7.5) using the same exact and one-position-off rules above.

💡 Example: You predict Hamilton for P1 in the race. He finishes P2. You get half of the P2 points — 9 points.
