How Scoring Works

Each race weekend you predict the top 3 in qualifying and the top 3 in the race. Points are awarded as follows:

✅ Exact match — you predicted a driver in the correct position, you get the full points for that position.

🔁 One position off — you predicted a driver one place out (e.g. predicted P1, they finished P2), you get half the points for where they actually finished.

❌ Two or more positions off — no points. If a driver doesn't finish in the top 3 at all, no points either.

💡 Example: You predict Hamilton for P1 in the race. He finishes P2. You get half of the P2 points — 9 points.
